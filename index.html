Welcome

These are model runs from the SWR MODFLOW model of 
the Mirabel Wohler Area. The Model is under development.
Each Run reflects the incoming stream inflows 
from Mark West Creek, Dry Creek, and the Russian River.

Currently the pumping from the collector wells do not 
reflect actual pumping at given time of model.They are 
inputs reused from 2009 model runs.
			
Other factors that need to be addressed:
	-Diversions and filling of infiltration ponds
	-Convert the observed RDS stages to dam elevations. 
		Currently the influence of stream stages from high 
		flow events are represented as changes in dam elevation.
	-Updated initial conditions (possibly re-run model 
		for ~10 days to establish initial conditions.
